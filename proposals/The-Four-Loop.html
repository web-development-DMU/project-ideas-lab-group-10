About the Application

SourceFlow is a web-based personal shopping request system. Users can submit sourcing requests, track progress, and update their details. The concierge team can manage requests and update statuses.

Services Offered

  • Private 1 on 1 consultation to understand needs
  • Quick sourcing of garments
  • Extra services such as gift wrapping
  • Price matching where applicable
