Brief introduction

Our application is called SourceFlow, created by the team The Four Loop.

SourceFlow is a web-based personal shopping request manager. Customers submit sourcing requests, track progress, and update their details. The concierge team reviews requests, updates statuses, and adds notes.

Users: who they are and what they can do

  • Customer: create a profile (sizes/brands/budget), submit a request, view status updates.
  • Concierge (admin): view requests, change status, add notes, and manage progress.

Why: It reduces time spent searching across sites and gives users a structured “concierge-style” flow.
